### Added
- Initial release with 66 tools across 12 categories.
- Categories: shell-scripts, cli-tools, git-utils, system-utils, file-utils, network-utils, dev-utils, text-utils, productivity, fun-utils, media-utils, security-utils.
- Top-level docs: README, CATEGORIES, CONTRIBUTING, CODE_OF_CONDUCT, SECURITY, LICENSE.

- `docs/` folder with GETTING_STARTED, PHILOSOPHY, FAQ, and ADVANCED_PIPES guides.
